Software Developer

As a software developer, you create applications and systems that run on computers or other devices. Skills required include programming languages like Python, Java, and C++.

Graphic Designer

Graphic designers create visual concepts to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. They use software like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Teacher

Teachers educate students in various subjects, preparing them for exams and life. Strong communication and patience are essential skills.

Doctor

Doctors diagnose and treat illnesses, requiring a deep knowledge of the human body and strong interpersonal skills.
